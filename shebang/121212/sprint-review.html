TIF2TTOM - Move relationship features to the complex table

  • In GDF, relationships and complex features are quite similar.
  • In GDF, maneuvers are being modelled as relationships, while in TTOM, they are being modelled as complex features.
  • This leads to anomalies in the GDF dataloader code.
  • It is not possible to simply translate GDF relations into TTOM associations.
  • Moving the GDF relationship features that are not pure TTOM associations to the GDF complex table, could simplify the GDF dataloader code a lot.
The following features where moved from relationship to complex.
  • Prohibited Maneuver, Priority Maneuver, Implicit Turn, Prohibited U Turn Maneuver, Restricted Maneuver, Calculated Prohibited Maneuver
  • Bifurcation, Signpost Information, Lane Connectivity, Image Maneuver
  • Grade separate crossing
  • Linear Assignment Maneuver
